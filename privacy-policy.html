Privacy Policy for Claude Chat Search

Last updated: May 17, 2025

Overview

Claude Chat Search is a Chrome extension that helps users search within Claude AI chat conversations. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information We Collect

The Claude Chat Search extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or chat content. All search operations happen entirely within your browser.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use your information in any way.

Data Storage

This extension uses Chrome's local storage API only to save your search settings and preferences. This data never leaves your browser.

Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your search preferences
  • Host permissions for Claude.ai: To enable the search functionality within Claude's website
  • Scripting/Tabs: To enable cross-conversation search capabilities (if implemented)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
